# Starter Guide
 
## Your Journey on Beam

Before you begin developing on Beam, there are several prerequisites you'll need to understand. Familiarity with the Solidity programming language, blockchain basics, and smart contract fundamentals are essential.

Once you're equipped with these, you're ready to set up your development environment. In the upcoming sections, we'll walk you through the process of getting your development toolkit ready, creating your first smart contract, testing it, and finally deploying it on the Beam network.

Remember, being a part of Beam means you're contributing to a collective vision. A vision of disrupting the gaming industry, driving innovation, and shaping the future of blockchain gaming. The community, the technology, and the potential for disruption are all here, waiting for you.
